# Servicios Open Editorial Rails Design

**Goal:** Refine the services card grid so it feels more minimal and editorial while preserving the client-approved copy, order, and section structure.

**Direction:** Move away from heavy boxed cards toward an open-layout system with subtle rails, more negative space, lighter dividers, and a restrained premium hover state. The visual language should stay native to The Monkeys: dark field, off-white typography, measured yellow accents, and strong editorial hierarchy.

**Approved choices:**
- Keep the same services, copy, and CTA position.
- Reduce solid card surfaces and rely on top rules, side rails, spacing, and type.
- Convert tags from pill-like chips into lighter editorial labels.
- Use a short, professional hover with slight lift, brighter rule, and improved text contrast.
- Keep the CTA card as the accent item, but cleaner and more integrated with the rest of the grid.
