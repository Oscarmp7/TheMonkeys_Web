# Nosotros Founder Editorial Plates Design

**Goal:** Refine the Nosotros page so it matches Home, Servicios, and Contacto while preserving the current structure, founder placeholders, and editorial tone.

**Direction:** Keep the page minimal and premium by removing watermark-style backgrounds, reducing boxed surfaces, and relying on typography, spacing, thin dividers, subtle glow, and near-imperceptible grain for atmosphere. The founder placeholders stay visible and intentional as part of the composition, not as hidden temporary assets.

**Approved choices:**
- Keep the current section order and base message.
- Remove large watermark/background lettering from the page.
- Alternate surfaces with solid color, very subtle white glow, and light grain only where it adds depth.
- Turn the team area into lighter founder editorial plates instead of heavy closed cards.
- Reduce the visual aggression of the yellow value split while keeping brand identity.
- Use restrained GSAP scroll motion only for reveal sequencing, founder activation, and subtle section emphasis.
