# Contacto Split Editorial Design

## Context

- The approved project reference is the current `Home`, not the original brief.
- `Contacto` should inherit the `Home` line while using the provided local HTML reference as inspiration.
- Placeholder content remains acceptable until the client supplies final assets.

## Chosen Direction

- Direction: `Split Editorial`
- Refinement layer: light `Premium Inquiry` restraint

## What We Keep From The Reference

- Strong dark / light split at the top of the page
- Dense contact-information block paired with a clean form surface
- FAQ continuation below the opening contact block
- Premium, high-contrast editorial rhythm

## What We Intentionally Reject

- Custom cursor behavior
- Emoji icons
- Template-like rounded card system
- Typography that drifts away from the current site voice

## Page Structure

### 1. Opening Contact Split

- Left panel: breadcrumb, eyebrow, large display headline, support copy, stacked contact routes, compact social / quick channels
- Right panel: form eyebrow, strong two-line title, supporting text, refined form inputs, submit CTA, response-time note

### 2. FAQ Continuation

- Keep FAQ below the opening split
- Refine spacing, hierarchy, and contrast to feel closer to the Home's editorial sections
- Preserve current accordion behavior and accessibility

## Typography

- Primary headline voice stays with `Anton`
- Secondary display moments can use `Barlow Condensed`
- Body text remains `Syne`
- Labels and metadata remain `DM Mono`

## Color Strategy

- Left panel: dark base with yellow accents and off-white content
- Right panel: off-white slab with navy emphasis and dark text
- FAQ: return to dark editorial ground with yellow highlights

## Interaction

- Stable hover states only
- No layout-shifting hover effects
- Reduced-motion users see all content with minimal animation

## Responsive Strategy

- Desktop: split layout
- Tablet: softened split with reduced side padding
- Mobile: stacked narrative first, form second, FAQ third
- No horizontal overflow at `375`, `768`, `1024`, or `1440`

## Scope

- In scope: `Contacto` page visual redesign and alignment
- Out of scope: `Home`, new business sections, replacing placeholders with assets not yet delivered
